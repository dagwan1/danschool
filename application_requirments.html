Application Requirements

Admissions Entry Requirements

DNPS offers rolling admissions and mid-year starts. Submit an application with applicable evaluations and records, and we'll promptly provide an admissions decision.

Application for All Year Groups: START AN APPLICATION

Teacher Evaluation: NOMINATE A TEACHER

Application Requirements by Year Group

PRE-NURSERY TO YEAR 1

  1. Application form
  2. Teacher evaluation via nomination form, if applicable
  3. Recent school or daycare report, if applicable

YEAR 2 TO YEAR 6

  1. Application form and fee
  2. Teacher evaluations (English, Mathematics, and School Leader)
  3. End-of-year reports from the two latest school years
  4. Online cognitive abilities test scheduled by DNPS Admissions

YEAR 2 TO YEAR 6

  1. Application form and fee
  2. Teacher evaluation via nomination form
  3. End-of-year reports from the two latest school years
  4. Online cognitive abilities test scheduled by BISW Admissions
