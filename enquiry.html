Welcome to Dan Nuery & Primary School Abuja

We’re delighted that you’re considering British International School Washington for your child’s education.

By completing the short form, we will respond back within 24hrs (excluding weekend) with suggested next steps. Whenever possible, we would recommend you to visit the school (physically or virtually) giving you and your family an opportunity to experience the school and answer any questions that you may have.

The best way to experience our school is to visit us, so please let us know your availability and we’ll do our best to accommodate you.

If you are experiencing technical issues, please contact our Admissions team at admissions@dnpsabuja.org.org call at +1 202 829 3700.
